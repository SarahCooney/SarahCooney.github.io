Sarah Cooney

cooneys@usc.edu

I am a PhD candidate studying Computer Science at the University of Southern California (USC) with a B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science from Saint Joseph's University (SJU). I am advised by Professor Barath. My research interests include the use of AI to study urban form, and the application of human computer interaction technologies to citizen-led urban planning and participatory design.

Projects

PatternPainter

A software system designed to allow everyday citizens to create designs for urban repair projects in their neighborhoods without the need for training or expert facilitation. Try the prototype here. (Enter any text as the MturkID.)

The CommYOUnity Data Project

A project to collect a dataset with photos of people's neighborhoods with captions describing what they love and what improvements they would make. Contribute here
